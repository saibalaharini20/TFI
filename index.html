Tollywood - Telugu Cinema Industry

What is Tollywood?

Tollywood is the Telugu-language film industry based in Hyderabad, Telangana. Famous for grand storytelling, action-packed dramas, and colorful musical numbers.

History

  • First Telugu film: Bhishma Pratigna (1921)
  • Raghupathi Venkaiah Naidu – Father of Telugu Cinema
  • Golden Era: 1950s–1980s with N.T. Rama Rao & Akkineni Nageswara Rao
